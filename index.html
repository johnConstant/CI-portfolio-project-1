Il Cibo

Using only the finest ingredients

Serving Cork since 1970

We pride ourselves on only using fresh, local and sustainable produce in our dishes. And with the best ingredients comes the best food which our chefs have been making for the last 50 years.

Located in the heart of Cork City, we have an amazing selection of ingredients from the land and sea and use only seasonal produce to put together some of Cork's favourite dishes. With a healthy mix of traditional and modern dishes we continue to be a favoutrite among locals and tourists so stop by and try a dish for yourself!

Our Team
