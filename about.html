Who we are

Having spent many a year working as a waiter himself, our owner John, takes the time to work with each and every member of staff to pass on all he has learned over the years and instill the same passion he has for the job in them.

Meet the Chef

Born in Southern Italy, Chef Giovanni Di Lorenzo is the youngest of 5 kids. He was the baby, which meant he was the lucky one who got to spend the most time with his Mama in the kitchen. It was there, in a tiny cottage in a mountain village, that he learned that the only thing that makes food so special is the amount of affection you cook into it. You see, in Italy, they use only the freshest ingredients to prepare food for the people they love and he has brought this etiquette with him to our little restaurant in Cork.

Our Team

We put the time into our people training them to be familiar with the cuisine and the areas of Italy from which it originates. We pride ourselves on having friendly, attentive waiters and barmen that not only know the food & drinks they serve, but love them too!
